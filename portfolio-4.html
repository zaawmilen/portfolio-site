A responsive weather app that provides users with real-time weather data, leveraging the OpenWeather API. The app offers dynamic content, including current weather conditions, forecasts, and location-based updates. With a clean, user-friendly interface, it adapts seamlessly to mobile and desktop devices, ensuring an intuitive experience for users.
